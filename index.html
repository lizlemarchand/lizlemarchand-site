I'm a certified NLP coach, business mentor, entrepreneur, and author — originally from New York, now living in France, and working with women around the world who are building something worth selling.

I spent 25 years in high-stakes sales and marketing — closing deals, leading teams, building revenue. And then I spent the next 15 years figuring out what was really going on underneath my own resistance, fear, and self-doubt. Spoiler: it wasn't a strategy problem.

My work blends sales expertise with NLP, CBT, EFT, and somatic practices — because you can have the best plan in the world, and if your internal programming says you don't deserve it, the plan won't work.
